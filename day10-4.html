Define a year as a "Superman year" whose films feature more Superman characters than Batman. How many years in film history have been Superman years?¶

In [8]:
superman_years = cast[cast['character'].str.contains('Superman|Batman')]
superman_vs_batman = superman_years.groupby('year')['character'].value_counts().unstack()
superman_years_count = (superman_vs_batman['Superman'] > superman_vs_batman['Batman']).sum()

print(f"The number of years in film history that have been 'Superman years' is {superman_years_count}.")

The number of years in film history that have been 'Superman years' is 1.

Build a plot with a line for each rank n=1 through n=3, where the line shows what fraction of that rank's roles were 'actor' roles for each year in the history of film.¶

In [ ]:
actor_actress_roles = cast[cast['type'].isin(['actor', 'actress'])]
roles_per_year_rank = actor_actress_roles.groupby(['year', 'Rank', 'type']).size().unstack()

for n in range(1, 4):
    roles_per_year_rank[f'Fraction of actor Roles (Rank {n})'] = roles_per_year_rank[f'actor (Rank {n})'] / (roles_per_year_rank[f'actor (Rank {n})'] + roles_per_year_rank[f'actress (Rank {n})'])

for n in range(1, 4):
    roles_per_year_rank[f'Fraction of actor Roles (Rank {n})'].plot(kind='line')

plt.xlabel('Year')
plt.ylabel('Fraction of actor Roles')
plt.title('Fraction of actor Roles for Each Rank (n=1 to n=3) Each Year')
plt.legend()
plt.show()
